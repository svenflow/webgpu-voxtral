How It Works

Voxtral uses a 3-stage pipeline. Text and voice embeddings are processed autoregressively by the backbone LLM, then a flow-matching transformer decodes audio codes, and finally a neural codec synthesizes the waveform.

Backbone
Ministral 3B LLM
text + voice → hidden states
Autoregressive
→
FM Transformer
Flow matching ODE
hidden → semantic + acoustic codes
32 Euler steps
→
Codec Decoder
Mimi neural codec
codes → 24kHz waveform
Conv + transformer
~8GB weights

50+ WGSL shaders

Pure WebGPU compute -- no WASM, no ONNX Runtime, zero dependencies

20 voices, 9 languages

EN, FR, ES, DE, IT, PT, NL, AR, HI with multiple voices per language

Instant reload

Weights cached in IndexedDB after first download. Subsequent loads are near-instant
